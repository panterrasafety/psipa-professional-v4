Verification definitions

Direct Controls: Controls that eliminate, isolate, physically prevent, or directly interrupt the high-energy hazard.

Indirect Controls: Controls that reduce exposure, improve awareness, or support safe execution when direct controls are in place.

Verified: The control is present, functioning, and confirmed at the work front.

Not Verified: The control is missing, ineffective, unclear, or cannot be confirmed. Treat as a deficiency or stop-work trigger where life-critical controls are affected.

SIF Review: Crew confirmation that Serious Injury/Fatality potential was discussed and that required critical controls are understood.
